 How It works

Advisors submit placement orders to ability placement markets. Advisors save time because algorithms sift through orders searching for the most attractive matches between talent seekers and candidates. Customer names remain anonymous in the market place until a talent seeker or candidate is ready to be introduced. Customers stay in control!

  Who we are

Placement Loop is a platform and ecosystem of suppliers serving ability placements markets. We provide a placement order management system, a collaboration tool, a curation mechanism, and business intelligence. We are the back office for people who want to help talent seekers and candidates identify best-fit quickly, accurately, and affordably. Everybody wins!

 Advise with us

Placement Loop provides a business opportunity for advisors to build a placement consultancy for rewards. Advisors use the platform for access to ability placement markets and the supplier ecosystem to get customers the commitments they are looking for quickly, accurately, and affordably. Advisors are our clients!
